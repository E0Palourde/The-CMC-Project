from RoA to the crusade comes Chara, the first fallen, and this time he did not deal with sans
some of his attacks are ... interesting
ssmash: it is a two-part attack: first the knife sticks itself into itself, causing damage to itself and to those close to it. The second part generates greater damage and allows chara to return to the first point (unlike other smashes, this one does not stop you during the smash_charge)
dsmash: a SAVE explosion that returns the impacted in 3 seconds
neutral b: enters attack mode (read ATTACK MODE section)
sideb: generates a shock wave ... multiplies in attack mode. Unfortunately even an unloaded lemon from megaman can block them
downb: still a counter but does not have roa's gaster blaster (returns projectiles
upb: throws his soul to teleport where he falls (has greater power in attack mode
FS: enter the true attack mode where the next attack separates the enemy's soul to have another area where it can be damaged
ATTACK MODE: boost your attack the closer the bar is to the center.
Hitting the perfect timing will cause X4.5 shockwave damage or the biggest boost of the soul.
Forcing a MISS (letting it go to the other corner) stuns you for a second if you are on the ground or puts you in failure if you are in the air.
Defense is reduced while in attack mode, chara's defense is reduced while in attack mode
Damage meter: as a reference to the original game, each attack generates a random number of numbers ranging between the power of damage and the offensive / defensive abilities that apply
taunt: it seems simple but it keeps 2 secrets
secret 1: you must be patient with normal taunt after winning LOVE
secret 2: if you eat chocolate ... for some reason chara remembers that she had another one in her pocket
